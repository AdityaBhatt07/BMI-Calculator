# BMI Calculator App

A simple **BMI (Body Mass Index) Calculator** built with **Streamlit**.  

Use this app to calculate your BMI based on your height and weight and see your BMI category.

---

## Features

- Interactive sliders to enter height (cm) and weight (kg)  
- Automatic BMI calculation  
- Displays BMI category:
  - Underweight: BMI less than 18.5  
  - Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9  
  - Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9  
  - Obesity: BMI greater than 30  

---

## How to Use

1. Open the app in your browser.  
2. Adjust the **height** and **weight** sliders.  
3. See your **BMI value** and corresponding category instantly.
